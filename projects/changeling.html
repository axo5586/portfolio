"ChangelingVR"

Website for a VR Game - Team of 4

Website

Worked in a web team of four during a co-op internship to make a website for a VR game. Started the website from scratch and got it to its current state in the span of three months.

Project direction was handled by a lead on a day to day basis, and base framework designs were developed in the beginning through a combination of team designs.

Screenshots:

Responsibilities:

Worked mainly on homepage interaction logic, design planning, and team page display logic. One of two primary coders in the project and worked off weekly feedback to revise and fix issues.

Worked off another team members' small class engine and developed interactions for each of the three characters: the mother, father and infant.

Utilized vue.js to create structure to display the website in conjunction with another team member, and focused mainly on the creation and logic handling of the team page. Read in data from the server that is created from filled out forms created by said team member and dynamically generate and display the teams on the team page.
